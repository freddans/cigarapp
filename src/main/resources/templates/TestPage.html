Cohiba.
Fidel Castro's Favorite

The mystical properties that surround the cigar are due to the Fidel Castro story.

Cohiba's origins are said to date back to the mid-1960s, in which a local torcedor was giving Fidel Castro's bodyguard hand-rolled cigars. Fidel Castro enjoyed them so much that he asked for them to be made for his own personal use. This meant that the El Laguito Factory began producing regular cigars containing the unbranded tobacco blend which would later be known as the world-famous Cohiba.
